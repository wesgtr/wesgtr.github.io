The target audience

The audience of this site are people who live in the region or come for some reason to these cities and need to know how is the climate to carry out their duties in the city.

Personas

The worker

Works in one of the cities and needs to move between them to carry out his duties

The Young

Who wants to know how the weather will be so he can go out with his friends to go to the lake, to the mall or just outdoor activities.

Scenarios

  • It will rain today? Will I be able to get to work on time? Need I to take an umbrella?
  • Will I take transit to deliver between cities? Is the snow blocking the passage?
  • Is the weather pleasant to go to the lake? Can we shower or just walk around?
  • Should I take a coat? What is the wind speed? Can i walk or go by car?
  • Is the sun too strong? Should I apply sunscreen?
